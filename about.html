Our Story

He worked as a second chef on board ships, where he spent months at sea preparing meals in fast-paced kitchen environments. During his off-duty periods, usually 3 months after 6 months of sailing, he decided to make the most of his time at home by starting a small business. That was when Signoff Coffee was born.
